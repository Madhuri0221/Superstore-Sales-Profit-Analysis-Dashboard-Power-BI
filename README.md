

## Problem
Business needed visibility into sales performance, order trends, and delivery delays across regions.

## Data
100,000+ sales and order records covering revenue, AOV, order volume, payments, and delivery metrics.

## Analysis & KPIs
Revenue, AOV, order volume, payment mix, regional performance, delivery delays.

## Outcome
Identified ~20% regional variation in performance and enabled drill-down based KPI monitoring for decision support.

## Tools
Power BI, DAX, Excel
