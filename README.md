# Superstore Sales & Profit Analysis Dashboard

## Problem
Business needed clear visibility into sales and profit performance across product sub-categories and regions to identify profit drivers and improvement opportunities.

## Data
Sample Superstore dataset with order-level sales, profit, discount, sub-category, region, and date information.

## Analysis & KPIs
Total Sales by Sub-Category
Sales % by Region
Total Profit
Average Discount
Monthly Profit Trend
Sub-Category contribution to overall profit

## Outcome
Identified sub-categories driving the majority of profit.
Highlighted high-sales segments with low margins due to discounting.
Revealed regional and monthly variation in profitability to support targeted actions.

## Tools
Power BI (Power Query, Data Modelling, DAX)
